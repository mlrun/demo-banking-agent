# General Customer FAQ

**Q1: How do I reset my online banking password?**
A1: Click “Forgot Password” on the login page, verify your identity via SMS or email, and follow the prompts to set a new password.

**Q2: What should I do if my debit card is lost or stolen?**
A2: Call our 24/7 Card Services at (555) 800-0000 to block the card immediately. A replacement will be sent within 3–5 business days.

**Q3: How can I dispute a transaction?**
A3: Log in to online banking, select the transaction, and click “Dispute.” Alternatively, call customer service at (555) 200-3000.

**Q4: What are the fees for ATM withdrawals at non-network ATMs?**
A4: Our bank charges \$2 per withdrawal plus any fees the ATM’s owner may impose.

**Q5: How do I set up direct deposit?**
A5: Provide your employer with your account number and routing number (123456789). You can find both on the “Account Details” page in online banking.

**Q6: How do I update my mailing address?**
A6: Go to Profile > Personal Information in online banking or visit any branch with proof of new address.
